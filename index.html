Join my bubble.

Adapting to a new normal doesn’t have to be confusing. Stay safe. Keep your bubble in the loop.

Get started

Exclusivity:

Each person can only join 1 bubble. If you receive multiple invitations to join different bubbles, you can only join 1.

Inclusivity:

When counting people in your bubble of 10, every family member living in each household included in the count must be included.

Awareness:

Your bubble is not invulnerable. If anyone in your bubble experiences symptoms of COVID-19 (self-assessment found here), immediately end the bubble and resume physical distancing.

Resilience:

The pandemic is not over. Remain vigilant in hand washing, wearing physically distancing with people outside of your bubble, and wearing masks.

Frequently asked questions

Can't find an answer? Don't hesitate to reach out!

What is a bubble?

Bubbles are social circles of up to 10 friends and family who can interact without physical distancing.

What is JoinMyBubble?

We exist to inform people about Ontario’s new social distancing policies, and to help people create their bubbles.
We hope this website accomplishes 3 main goals:
1) Letting people know who else is in their bubble.
2) Letting people become aware when they are potentially involved with multiple bubbles.
3) Ensuring that people joining a bubble understand the risks and rules necessary for contributing to a safe bubble.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes. Data will never be used for commercial purposes. Emails will be used to provide additional recommendations if guidelines with regards to bubbles change. Users can unsubscribe at any time.

Are you affiliated with the government?

No, we are a group of medical students at the University of Toronto. DM's are open, though.
